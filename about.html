Our Spiritual Journey

Guiding pilgrims to sacred destinations since 2010

Our Mission

To facilitate transformative spiritual journeys that connect travelers with the sacred heritage of humanity's most revered destinations. We believe that pilgrimage is not just travel, but a profound opportunity for personal growth and cultural understanding.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted provider of spiritual travel experiences, creating bridges between cultures and faith traditions through meaningful, authentic pilgrimages that respect local customs and promote global understanding.

Mr. Tang

Founder & Spiritual Director

With over 15 years of experience in spiritual tourism and interfaith dialogue, Mr. Tang founded Pilgrim Paths with a simple yet profound vision: to create journeys that nourish the soul while respecting the sacred traditions of each destination.

His personal pilgrimages to over 50 sacred sites across six continents have given him unique insights into what makes a spiritual journey truly transformative.

"True pilgrimage is not about reaching a destination, but about the transformation that occurs along the way. It's in the journey itself that we discover our deepest selves."
