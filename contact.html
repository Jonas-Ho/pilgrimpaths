Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for the best availability, especially for popular pilgrimage seasons.

What's included in the tour packages?

All packages include accommodation, guided tours, most meals, and spiritual activities. Flights and personal expenses are additional.

Are there physical requirements?

Some destinations require moderate physical activity. We offer different difficulty levels and can accommodate most mobility needs.

What if I need to cancel?

We offer flexible cancellation policies with full refunds up to 60 days before departure. See our terms for details.
